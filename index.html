About Al-Barakat-ul-Makiyyah

Discusess the miracles of reciting durud sharif

Al Barkatul Makkiyah, also known as Barakat Makkiyah, is a significant Islamic book authored by Shaykh Musa Ruhani Bazi. This work is notable for its compilation of 805 names of the Prophet Muhammad (peace be upon him), along with various Durood Sharif(salutations) that are believed to bring blessings and facilitate ease in difficulties for those who recite them

Hizb-ul-Azam

Focuses on the life and practices of the Prophet and Durud Sharif on Muhammad (PBUH)

Hizbul-Azam is a revered compilation of supplications (duas) and prayers (wazifas) derived from the Quran and Hadith. Compiled by the great Islamic scholar Mullah Ali Al-Qari, this collection is widely used by Muslims around the world for spiritual guidance and protection. The prayers in Hizbul-Azam cover various aspects of life.

Durood Wa Salam

A path to peace and prosperity
Durood Sharif, a beautiful invocation of blessings upon the Prophet Muhammad (PBUH), holds immense spiritual significance in Islam. Reciting Durood or Salawat brings countless rewards, fostering a deep connection with Allah and His beloved Prophet. It is said to invoke peace, mercy, and forgiveness, and carries the promise of blessings in both this world and the hereafter.
